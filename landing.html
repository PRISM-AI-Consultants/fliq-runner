What is FLIQ?

FLIQ measures six dimensions of financial thinking in children - not right or wrong answers, but how they naturally approach money decisions.

Financial Literacy Intelligence Quotient

Every child has a unique financial personality. FLIQ helps them discover it through play.

Saving

Does your child naturally set aside for later? This dimension reveals their instinct to accumulate and protect resources.

Patience

Can they wait for a bigger reward? This tracks delayed gratification - one of the strongest predictors of financial success.

Needs vs. Wants

Can they tell the difference between what they need and what they want? This is the foundation of smart spending.

Sharing

How generous are they with resources? This dimension captures their natural inclination toward giving and community.

Risk Awareness

Are they cautious or adventurous with choices? Understanding risk tolerance early helps build balanced financial habits.

Budgeting

Can they allocate limited resources wisely? This tracks how children distribute coins across competing priorities.

For Grown-Ups

Designed with Parents & Educators in Mind

FLIQ Runner is built on research-backed financial literacy principles, wrapped in a game kids actually want to play.

No Child Fails

FLIQ is a behavioral profile, not a test. Every outcome is framed positively, celebrating each child's unique approach to financial thinking.

Data-Driven Insights

See real patterns in how your child makes financial decisions. Understand their strengths and where gentle guidance could help.

Ages 6-10, All Visual

Curriculum Aligned

Our Mission

Part of the Wealth Wise Kids Project

FLIQ Runner was created as part of the Wealth Wise Kids initiative, a program dedicated to promoting financial literacy among children of color ages 6-10. We believe that early exposure to financial concepts - through play, not pressure - builds the foundation for lifelong financial confidence.

"Bridging Gaps, Empowering Futures"

Every child deserves to understand money, regardless of their background. FLIQ Runner meets kids where they are: in the game, in the fun, in the moment of discovery.
